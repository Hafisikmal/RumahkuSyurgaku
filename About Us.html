Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato’ Seri Haji Harun Idris

[21 Julai 1924 - 19 Oktober 2003]

Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato' Seri Haji Harun was born on July 21, 1924. He was the founder of Rumah Bakti Hulu Kelang. Rumah Bakti Hulu Kelang now is known as Rumah Bakti Dato Harun. As a tribute to Y.Bhg late Dato' Haji Harun Idris, the members of the United Nations Educational Orphan Selangor had agreed to change the name Rumah Bakti Hulu Kelang (RBHK) to Rumah Bakti Datuk Harun (RBDH). Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato' Seri Haji Harun was passed away on October 19, 2003. Al-Fatihah.

HISTORY OF "RUMAH BAKTI"

On 16 August 1966, a group of community activists and senior Selangor State Government officials successfully established and registered the "Selangor Orphan Education Organization" under Registration Number ARS/Sel/50/66 out of a sense of responsibility and sympathy for orphans. Start with the approval of this registration, the Committee has made strides toward its goal of formation. According to approval document number PHT.G.2/139 /75, the Selangor State Government has granted a plot of land in Mukim Hulu Kelang (HS.231) with an area of 4.8 acres for the services of Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato' Haji Harun Idris and his wife Y.Bhg Datin Salmah Suleiman. Well, to ensure the success of this project, Y.Bhg Allahyarham Dato' Haji Harun Idris has been appointed as Chairman of this Organization.Read More
