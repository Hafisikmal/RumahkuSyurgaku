LET'S INFAQ

According to the dictionary of the language and library board, infaq is a gift in the form of money or property that is spent for goodness and welfare solely to gain the pleasure of Allah S.W.T. If we pursue the approach of Tarbiyah Ilahiyyah through the Qur'an, we will find how Allah Almighty, the Wisest, makes infaq an exercise of the soul and the beginning to bear the spirit of jihad in the path of Allah. Infaq or donation or charity is a way that Allah Taala educates the human heart so that it is purified from feelings of greed and pride so that it can finally be open to accept greater responsibility.

THE SWEETNESS OF CHARITY

Islam is a religion that cares and loves orphans. This is reflected through the qudwah hasanah (a good example) by the Prophet SAW when he treats orphans with love and kindness. This is because Prophet SAW, who once grew up as an orphan, understands the psychology and feelings that play in the minds of orphans.The Qur'an clearly mentions the affairs of orphans. Allah SWT said which means,"And they ask you (O Muhammad) about the orphans. Say that doing good to them (orphans) is a good thing." (Surah Al-Baqarah verse 220). Allah SWT also forbids being rude to orphans. This is explained in surah ad-Dhuha verse 9, where Allah SWT says which means. "Therefore, as for orphans, then you should not be rude to them,"
