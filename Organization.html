RBDH ORGANIZATION CHART

All policies and management of the RBDH are determined by a body known as the Selangor Orphan Education Organization Working Council. Members of the Working Council Committee are appointed from among individuals who are members of this Organization. Members of the Working Council Committee are appointed from among the members through the General Meeting every 2 years. The 40th General Meeting which met on 03 July 2022 has officially appointed the Working Council Members for the 2022-2024 session as above.
